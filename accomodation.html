Rooms & Suites

Our Bedrooms

Our 19 beautiful bedrooms are equipped with in-house essentials such as local tea and coffee, Scottish Fine Soaps, soft cotton sheets and luxury modern bathrooms by Porcelanosa.

Rooms Include
  • Luxury beds
  • Dressing tables and writing desk
  • Sofa beds, cots and pull out spare beds in some rooms
  • Flat screen Smart TV with blue-tooth speakers
  • Coffee machines and a selection of speciality teas
  • Luxury bathrooms by Porcelanosa
  • Beautiful decor to make you feel as relaxed as possible!

Suites

A luxury night away to Loch Lomond and The Trossachs needs luxury at it’s best!

Relax into the most amazing super king bed made up with luxury linens and indulgent modern furnishings. Perfect for guests who want it all, these carefully decorated suites will bring you ultimate rest and relaxation.

Classic King

Designed for the luxury traveller, spacious with plenty of room and a light and airy feel, our classic king rooms have been created to allow you to feel the comfort of home and relax.

Classic Double

Our luxury classic double provides the perfect space to relax when exploring the beauty of Loch Lomond, Glasgow and The West. Enjoy this lovely room and en-suite bathroom featuring a luxury bathroom with double headed rain shower to leave you feeling refreshed and alive each morning.
