Welcome to The Artemis Hotel

A warm welcome awaits you at The Artemis Hotel. Built in 1798, this grand hotel is luxury haven minutes from the banks of Loch Lomond and within close proximity to Glasgow and Glasgow International Airport. Experience a country style bespoke hotel in a listed building filled with history and character. We feature 19-luxury suites and bedrooms, The Castle Suite wedding and events venue, The Rock Bar for all day dining as well as a contemporary Scottish restaurant (COMING 2018).

Luxury Accomodation

Each of our 19-rooms are equipped with essentials such as tea and coffee, Scottish Fine Soap toiletries, beautiful bedding, luxury modern bathrooms and the best technology.

Just for you, we have added special luxuries so you can experience the very best accommodation in Glasgow and the West of Scotland!

Luxury Wedding Venue

Set beneath the backdrop of the impressive Dumbuck Hill. The Castle Suite is a superb setting for your wedding ceremony, accommodating up to 120 guests.

Once you’ve said ‘Aye-do’ guests can socialise in your own private courtyard, on the lawn or enjoy The Rock Bar while photography is taking place. Our kitchen will create the perfect meal using the finest Scottish ingredients.

Exceptional Dining

A casual and informal space with no compromise on the quality of our food. We are open all day for casual dining making The Artemis Hotel the perfect place for all the family, special occasions and a a great gathering place!

Using only fresh, locally sourced ingredients, our chefs create mouth-watering dishes for all tastes. We serve fresh local produce at its best by keeping it simple and delicious.

Special Events

The Artemis Hotel is a well-known and admired venue in Dumbarton, Glasgow and The West of Scotland.

Our facilities offer numerous size rooms to host that special occasion. We host a variety of events such as Banquet Dinners, Funerals and Christenings, Birthday Parties to Corporate Away-Days, Fashion Shows and Ceilidhs and with accommodation on site and ample parking makes The Artemis Hotel a superior choice for all.

Things To Do

Stretching across central Scotland from east to west, the landscapes of Loch Lomond and The Trossachs. Sandwiched between the Firth of Forth and the Firth of Clyde, the area is woven together by rivers, canals and lochs, meaning that there are plenty of water-based activities to enjoy and lush scenery to take in.

The Artemis Hotel is perfect for the adventure traveller, walkers, cyclists and families who love the outdoors!
